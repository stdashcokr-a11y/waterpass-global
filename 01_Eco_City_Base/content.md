**3 Key Benefits:**

*   **Superior Flood Prevention & Water Cycle Restoration:** 
    Our paving blocks go far beyond standard regulatory minimums.     Traditionally, increasing block porosity for better water flow compromises strength. Utilizing optimized aggregate grading and binder mixture technologies, our blocks guarantee a high flexural strength of 5.0 MPa or more [3].
